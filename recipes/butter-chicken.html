Butter Chicken

Description

One of my favorite Indian dinners is this crockpot butter chicken. This dish was created by a friend for my family, and when we had it, we fellÂ in love! I don't think I've ever had a better butter chicken. I enjoy putting it in the slow cooker in the morning and coming home to the aroma of warm Indian spices... Serve with warm naan bread and basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons vegatable oil
  • 4 large skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into bitsized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon curry paste
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
